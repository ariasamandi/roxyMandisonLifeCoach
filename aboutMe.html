About Me

Hi my name is Roxy and I’m a dedicated certified life coach (CLC) specializing in empowering women to live purposeful, meaningful lives and relationship coaching. I have been a mental health therapist for over 2 years and have a Master’s in Social Work. I’ve always cared about helping others and creating a better world.


I grew up in Los Angeles by the beach and now live with my husband in Cupertino, CA. I enjoy going to the beach, hiking, and traveling on my free time. In my early 20’s I volunteered in a country called Kosovo where I facilitated a community builiding program on the grassroots level.


My coaching approach is centered on creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your highest aspirations, identify obstacles, and develop strategies to overcome them. Together, we will work on transforming your challenges into opportunities for growth and creating a life that truly resonates with who you are.
